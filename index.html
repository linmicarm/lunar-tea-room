Hi, I'm Selene

I've been reading tarot for twelve years, since my grandmother handed me her worn deck and told me it was never about fortune. It was just about paying closer attention.

That's still how I read. Not to fix your future in place, but to help you slow down, notice what you already sense, and take the next step with a steadier hand. Every reading is calm, unhurried, and free of judgment.
